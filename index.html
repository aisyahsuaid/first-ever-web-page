Welcome to Sanrio Land!!

Sanrio is a Japanese company that designs and produces kawaii (cute) characters of Japanese popular culture


Pompompurin 🌟

ポムポムプリン (Pomupomupurin) is a good-natured Golden Retriever dog


Hello Kitty 🎀

ハローキティ (Harō Kitī) is a female white Japanese Bobtail Cat with a red bow.


Cinnamoroll 🍄

シナモロール (Shinamorōru) is a little chubby male dog with white fur and curly tail that resembles a cinnamon roll.


Kuromi ⛓

クロミ (Kuromi) is a white rabbit or imp-like creature with a pink skull on the front and devil's tail at the back.
